About Us


Cubicescape is a story base escape room franchise that originated from Shanghai, China in December of 2012. Our mission is to use escape room as a medium for story telling to deliver a high quality immersive entertainment experience. We specialize in game design, game research, puzzle props manufacturing and retail operation. Our escape rooms are present in all major cities in China and recently we have opened new escape rooms in London, UK. We are extremely excited to open our first escape rooms in U.S in San Jose.

We believe that a great escape room experience is beyond just puzzle solving. In our rooms, we take a lot of pride to craft and blend our stories with the puzzles, much like movie watching. The puzzles you and your team solve together will move the story forward and be prepared for a fun and emotional ride that may end up in tears or surprise.

Many our rooms have been featured in international media like Global Times and have been awarded the best escape room award in China in 2017.
