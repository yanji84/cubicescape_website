Who Am I

Moderate
2-8
Players
60
Minutes

You are suffering from severe memory loss. After leaving hospital, you have been tormented by the same nightware night after night. This morning, you woke up in bed as usual, with headache, wondering where you are. The room looks familiar, and from your diaries, you realize that there is a trail of delicately planted clues that will help you recover your memory. Following the trail of clues left by yourself, the answer starts to become more clear. Will you be able to remember your past? What is the meaning behind your nightmares? A secret lies in your past that is waiting for you to discover...
