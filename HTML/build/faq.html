FAQs


If your question isn’t answered here, feel free to contact us at info@cubicescape.us

What is an Escape Room?

An escape game is a real-life experience where participants are locked in a room and must use elements of the room, the group’s collective knowledge, and wits to discover clues, solve puzzles, and escape and/or find the missing item within a set amount of time.

What is story based escape room?

Traditional escape rooms have a barebone story that is usually neglected as part of the gameplay. While it is fun to get down to the puzzles, we believe that the best escape room experience comes with a compelling storyline. We take great pride in crafting the story that blends with the puzzle solving to deliver a fun and immersive experience.

How long does the whole experience take?

Our rooms' time limit is 60 or 90 minutes to play.

What happens if we are late?

To ensure the game starts on time, we advise the whole team to arrive at least 15 minutes prior to the booking. Should you be running late, please let us know as soon as possible. If anyone is slightly delayed, we will do our best to start the game with all the members present. However, if the next slot is booked for another team, being late will mean less time for your game. Whenever possible and under special circumstances, you may be able to reschedule your booking on site.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

No cancellations or refunds are allowed once a session is booked, however, you are able to reschedule free of charge three days in advance. For shorter notice, additional charges may apply. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Is there an age limit?

There are no age restrictions. However, children below 16 must be accompanied by an adult.

Is parking easy?

Free parking is available in the same plaza that is dedicated to the 2470 Berryessa suites. During busy hours, more parking is available at the shopping mall across the street.

Can I buy tickets as gift for others?

Absolutely! We do sell gift certificates. Simply go to Book Now!", select Gift Voucher and we will send an email with the gift code to your friend and/or family.

Can I host a team building or private event?

Absolutely! We will cater to your needs and decorate the space to make it look just the way you want it, be it a team offsite or a birthday party! We will help you to craft an unforgettable experience! Please send us more information at event@cubicescape.us
